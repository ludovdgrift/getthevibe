Frequently asked questions

Good to know

When does it launch?
We're starting soon in Amsterdam. Join the waitlist and you'll be among the first in.
How is it different from Google Maps or reviews?
Reviews tell you how a place once was. VibeNow shows you the spots real people — and your friends — genuinely recommend right now, with a community vibe score instead of anonymous stars.
How does the vibe score work?
When someone marks a place, others can confirm or disagree with the vibe. Those votes add up into a community-driven score, so the genuinely good places rise to the top.
What does it cost?
Joining the waitlist and using VibeNow is free.
What about my privacy?
VibeNow is about places, not people. The map shows the vibe of a spot — never where any individual is live. You choose what you share, and with whom.
Who is VibeNow for?
For anyone who's tired of guessing where to go — and wants to discover, save and share places that actually have a vibe.
