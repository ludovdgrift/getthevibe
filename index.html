Frequently asked questions

Good to know

When does it launch?
We're starting soon. Join the waitlist and you'll be among the first in.
How is it different from Google Maps or reviews?
Reviews are anonymous strangers, and a Maps list gets lost and isn't really shared. VibeNow is the places your friends genuinely recommend, in one shared list your whole group can find again.
What does it cost?
Joining the waitlist and using VibeNow is free.
What about my privacy?
VibeNow is about places, not people - it never shows where anyone is. You choose what you save and which lists you share, and with whom.
Who is VibeNow for?
Anyone with a group of friends who keep losing track of the places they love - and want one shared list to save and find them again.
